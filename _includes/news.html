February 5th 2016:

Veteran Rocker Damon Kelly To Record Bowie Song Tribute

LOS ANGELES, /PRNewswire/ - With two successful International album releases under their belt, the acclaimed rock group, "LOVE N' REVENGE," fronted by rock veteran Damon Kelly, is going into the Red V Studios in New York to record the David Bowie classic "Fame" as a tribute to the late music icon. Kelly, who has a long history in melodic rock, will release the song internationally in Spring 2016.
